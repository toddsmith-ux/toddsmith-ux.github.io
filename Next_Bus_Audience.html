Target Audience

Demographics

The NextBus app is targeted at people who rely on the Chapel Hill Transit System, specifically those who wish to obtain additional information about bus information beyond the information available at bus stops (i.e. paper schedules and electronic billboards). The age of users ranges between 10-90, from young children traveling with their own smart phone to seniors who have decades of experience relying on the bus system prior to the introduction of NextBus.

As NextBus is a technology that accompanies a public service, the accessibility profile varies tremendously. This audience requirement has implications in the design of the NextBus mobile app; specifically, the features available and usability of those features must accommodate those with visual, audio, motor, and psychological impairments.

User Motivations

In terms of motivations, we note that users use the NextBus mobile application either 1) before arriving at the target bus stop or 2) to confirm any discrepancies in a bus arrival time, usually as a result of delay. Those in the first group often wish to avoid long wait times at the bus stop and therefore have a focus on timing the arrival of the bus exactly. Users in the second group are physically present at the bus stop but use NextBus mobile to understand how much longer they must wait, why they may be experiencing a delay, or select an alternative route “on the fly” that better suits their travel.
