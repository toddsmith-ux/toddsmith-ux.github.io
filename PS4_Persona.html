Personas

To construct the personas, I conducted two user interviews. I used a convenience sample to find people who were online gamers. I included people who play other gaming systems to consider needs that were not exclusive to PS4. Afterwards, I created two personas.

Casual Cassie

My first persona, Casual Cassie, represents a more infrequent gamer. Cassie plays PS4 casually on the weekends. Cassie is very passionate about expanding the identity of gamers to be more inclusive towards women. Thus, Cassie is a member of a female gamer group and participates in the groupâ€™s events. Cassie could use this proposed app to maximize the amount of time for gaming.

Figure 2: Casual Cassie Persona

Gerald the Gamer

My second persona, Gerald the Gamer, represents a passionate, avid gamer. Gerald plays PS4 every day after work. Gerald strives to show that gamers are not one dimensional people. Therefore, Gerald participates in numerous activities outside of gaming. On the contrary, Gerald is significantly involved in the gamer community and often watches YouTube videos about gaming.
